"Recipe to make Coffee !"

Ingredients:

  1. Coffee powder
    • Any coffee powder
  2. Milk
  3. Sugar
  4. Water



Steps to make Coffee:
  1. Pour milk into a pan and heat it.
  2. Take a table spoon of coffee powder and put it inside the cup.
  3. Add sugar to the cup.
  4. Add the boiled milk into the cup and mix it well until the powder is liquified.
  5. Serve it hot.
